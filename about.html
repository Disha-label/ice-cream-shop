About Us

Welcome to dhi-iceCream!

Where every scoop tells a story and every visit is a celebration of flavor.

Founded in 1989, dhi-iceCream began as a dream shared by ice cream enthusiasts who wanted to create a haven for those who cherish the simple pleasures of life. Our passion for ice cream goes beyond just crafting delicious treats; it’s about creating lasting memories and moments of joy. From our humble beginnings in a small shop to becoming a beloved destination, our journey has been filled with laughter, creativity, and a whole lot of ice cream!

Our Craft

At dhi-iceCream, we believe that great ice cream starts with great ingredients. That’s why we prioritize sourcing only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, from local farms to artisanal suppliers. Each batch is handcrafted in small quantities to ensure the perfect texture and flavor. Our diverse range of flavors – from timeless classics like Vanilla and Chocolate to innovative creations like Mango Chili Sorbet and Lavender Honey – caters to every palate and whim.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to sustainability and community engagement. We strive to minimize our environmental footprint by using eco-friendly packaging and supporting local farmers. Our team is passionate about giving back, whether it’s through partnerships with local charities or hosting community events. We believe in the power of ice cream to bring people together, and we’re committed to making a positive impact in our community.

Join Us

Whether you’re enjoying a scoop on a hot summer day, indulging in a sundae with friends, or picking up a cake for a special occasion, we’re here to make every moment a little sweeter. We invite you to visit one of our locations, explore our menu online, or follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest flavors, events, and promotions.

Our Mission

To craft delightful, high-quality ice creams that bring joy and excitement to every customer, one scoop at a time.

Our Promise

At dhi-iceCream, we promise to deliver fresh, flavorful ice cream made with love, using premium ingredients and served with a smile. We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home and every visit is a delightful experience.

We can’t wait to serve you!
